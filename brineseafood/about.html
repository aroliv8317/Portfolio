The concept of Brine is largely inspired by Executive Chef, Marc Taft's, time in California and Chef de cuisine, David Conolly's, growing up in Maine. Eating at Brine gives you the feeling of being on a beach trip without having to leave home.
All seafood served at Brine is sustainably sourced according to the Monterey Bay Watch Organization. This allows them to serve you the freshest seafood possible while being globally conscious. You can feel great knowing how and where your dinner was caught.
